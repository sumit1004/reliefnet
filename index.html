How It Works

  1. Report Needs: Community members or volunteers submit urgent needs with photos, videos, and location details.
  2. Community Validation: Other users upvote or confirm the need, helping prioritize the most critical cases.
  3. Resource & Volunteer Matching: ReliefNet matches available resources and volunteers to the highest priority needs using smart algorithms.
  4. Track & Coordinate: All actions are tracked transparently, and updates are shared in real-time with all stakeholders.
  5. Recognition: Volunteers and contributors earn badges for their efforts, encouraging ongoing participation.
ReliefNet ensures no need goes unnoticed and every helping hand is utilized efficiently.
